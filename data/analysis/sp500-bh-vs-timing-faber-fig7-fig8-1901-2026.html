Methodology

Timing rule: At each month-end compare price to 10-month SMA. Price > SMA → invested. Price ≤ SMA → exit to T-bills. Signal at close of t−1, applied to return of month t (1-month lag).

SMA warm-up: Full-year 1900 prices used to seed the SMA. First signal = Jan 1901. No look-ahead bias.

Cash: FRED TB3MS 3-month T-bill (1934–). Pre-1934 cash = 0% (T-bill data unavailable).

Data: Shiller monthly S&P 500 total return (1871–2013) spliced with SPY ETF adjusted close.

Ulcer Index: RMS of drawdown series (%). Penalises depth and duration. Lower is better.

No costs, taxes or leverage.
